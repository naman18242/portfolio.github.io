About Me

Greet Good morning. I would like to introduce myself, My name is Naman Gulati. I am 24 years old. I live in Ranibagh of Delhi. As far as my qualification is concerned. I completed my 12th CBSE BOARD from M.M Public School with 70%. I am pursuing my graduation from the University of Delhi. I have Completed my GWDD Course (Graphic Web Design and Development) from Arena Animation,Pitampura. I'm looking for a job where I can learn new things and in-plant it practically.

Rather than create websites on a page-by-page basis, I instead consider each element of your screen-based designs as separate components that can exist elsewhere on a website; outside of the context of your design compositions.

With these components I produce a style guide that acts as a document for a reusable and maintainable code base. All involved in a project can rely on a style guide to understand a project’s language and produce a more consistent user experience.
